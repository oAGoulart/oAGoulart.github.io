I consider the privacy of my visitors to be extremely important. This privacy policy document describes in detail the types of personal information that are collected and recorded by this site and how they are used.

Log Files

Like many other sites, this site makes use of log files. These files merely logs visitors to the site - usually a standard procedure for hosting companies and a part of hosting services's analytics. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Personal Data

This site does not collect any kind of personally identifiable information and you should not send any personal or confidential information to this site. If you were asked such information by this site, contact me immediatly (contact information below).

Cookies and Web Beacons

This site only uses security related cookies provided by CloudFlare (learn more about it here), which are absolutely necessary for supporting this site's security features. Those cookies are used to identify individual clients behind a shared IP address and apply security settings on a per-client basis. Although, they do not store any personally identifiable information.
